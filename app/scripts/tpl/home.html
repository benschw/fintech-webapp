Funraising.

FUNDER is a site we built for FinTech Hackathon that demonstrates a way for non-profits to gamify their online fund raising efforts.

Splitting the profit with the donors is a way to create excitement and a viral aspect that isn't usually present in a typical online donations drive.

How does it work?.

Non-profit organizations can post items for their followers to buy... with a twist. As soon as an item is purchased, it goes back up for purchase at an increased price. If another follower purchases the now-higher-priced item, the first follower is paid back, and splits the profit with the NPO.

When the market closes (say, after a week) the items are shipped to the last purchasers.
